About the Journey

Passion for the cosmos drives every exploration

Welcome to Universe & Beyond, where curiosity meets the infinite expanse of space. I'm Shankar Neupane, dedicated to unraveling the mysteries that surround us, from the smallest quantum particles to the largest galactic structures.

This journey began with a simple question: "What lies beyond the stars we see?" That question has evolved into a mission to share the wonder, beauty, and scientific rigor of space exploration with fellow cosmic enthusiasts.

Every video, research paper, and mission log entry represents hours of investigation, verification, and passion for accuracy. Join me as we explore the universe together, one discovery at a time.
